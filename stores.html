Gentle Monster Aoyama Flagship store offers a new sensory experience through a deep exploration of the human psyche. Under the theme “3.0,” the space moves away from the previous powerful and heavy imagery, delivering an approachable yet avant-garde and sophisticated ambiance. Upon entering the store, visitors first encounter the three “Giant Head Kinetic Objects” that reflect the new worldview of Gentle Monster. The expressions that seem absorbed in deep thought, the softly blinking eyelids, and the movements of the eyes that seem aware of each other, encapsulate the exploration of human thoughts and emotions, relationship between people, and the inner self. The exploration of the inner self extends to the second floor, expressed through the space’s wild and rough mood. The overwhelming “Chimpanzee” object, along with the sparkling “Star” object, meet the light infiltrating the store to deliver a dazzling sensation that emulates the morning sun.

The Gentle Monster Starfield Hanam store, themed ‘Self Similarity,’ reinterprets the mathematical concept through visual and spatial elements. Inspired by the mathematical theory of self-similarity, the various artwork, images, and installations in the store represent the infinite possibility of ‘expansion’ through repeating geometric structures, such as fractals, Mandelbrot sets, and Julia sets. The kinetic installations located at the façade, repeatedly expanding and contracting, and artwork that expresses infinite proliferation show Gentle Monster’s unique and artistic perspective on the concept of self-similarity.
