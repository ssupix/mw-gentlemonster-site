The flood of discrete images from the digital space is reintegrated to express a new self through one’s own ‘Awakening.’ The various artwork and installations meticulously exhibit intense contrasts in color and material, further emphasizing the disintegration.rnHumans appearing in video artwork portray the process of self-actualization within the reckless inundation of data.
